Reflection on the readings:

I feel that this reading was a vortex of what ifs and but maybes. I feel that decolonizing technology in this year and age is a difficult issue to change because it has taken many years and wars to get the information that we have now. With that information, it has built the foundation of what we are today. I’m not suggesting that colonialism was a good thing but rather it was the only information people had to work with. Now, that there is some freedom of speech, we can add to technological advances by “cultivating conversation” (Mignolo and Walsh: 2018). It is impossible to rewrite the past or change everyone’s perspective, but we can use technology to educate the new generation and create awareness of the truth. We have the skills, facilities, and the power to bring out the truth from culturally reliable sources.

Reference:

Walsh, C, and Mignolo, W. 2018. Decoloniality Concepts, Analytics and Praxis.
